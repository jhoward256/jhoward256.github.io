Classic Vanilla Cake

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 30-35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour and baking powder.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk.
  6. Stir in vanilla extract.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let cool before frosting or serving.
